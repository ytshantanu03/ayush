About ESTPL

ESTPL is a scientific enterprise under the Scientist Entrepreneurship Scheme of the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR), formed by scientists of CSIR-National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (NEERI). ESTPL intends to carry on the business of providing innovative technical solutions for environmental applications including:

  • Wastewater Treatment
  • Sewage Treatment
  • Solid Waste Management
  • Air Pollution Monitoring and Control
  • Water Treatment
  • Consultancy Services for Environmental Applications

ESTPL is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company and has acquired various patented technologies from IITs, educational institutes, and research organizations like BARC & NEERI.
